CURATORS

HEAD CURATOR

DR. SARANYAPHAT BOONMEE

Dr. Saranyaphat Boonmee is a Researcher at the Terrestrial and Aquatic Fungi Laboratory of the Center of Excellence in Fungal Research, Mae Fah Luang University. Her research works focused mostly on freshwater and hyphomycetes fungi of Dothideomycetes in different habitats in Thailand.

MANAGING CURATORS

SAJINI CHANDRASIRI

Sajini is currently pursuing his PhD degree in Biological Sciences at the Center of Excellence in Fungal Research, Mae Fah Luang University, Thailand under the supervision and co-supervision of Prof. KD Hyde and Dr. Saranyaphat Boonmee, respectively. Her research focuses on the taxonomy, phylogeny and evolution of freshwater fungi.

CURATORS
